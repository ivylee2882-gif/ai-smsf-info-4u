Top Questions About SMSF

Who Can Set Up an SMSF?

  • Who is eligible? Australian residents over 18, not bankrupt, can set up an SMSF. ATO.
  • Can minors be members? Minors can be members under legal guardian control. SMSF Association.
  • Max members? Up to six members, typically family or business partners. ATO.
  • Trustee requirements? All members must be trustees or directors of a corporate trustee. SMSF Association.
  • Can non-residents join? Non-residents can join if compliant with ATO rules. ATO.
  • Disqualified persons? Convicted fraudsters or bankrupts cannot be trustees. ATO.
  • Corporate vs individual trustee? Corporate trustees offer liability protection, individual trustees are simpler. SMSF Association.
  • Can employees join? Employees can join if unrelated to employer trustee. ATO.
  • Age limits? No upper age limit for setting up an SMSF. SMSF Association.
  • Financial knowledge needed? No formal qualifications, but financial literacy is recommended. ATO.

Why Set Up an SMSF?

  • Control? Offers direct control over investment choices and strategy. SMSF Association.
  • Flexibility? Allows diverse investments like property, shares, or crypto. ATO.
  • Cost-effective? Can be cost-effective for balances over $500,000. SMSF Association.
  • Tax benefits? Offers tax strategies like franking credits, deductions. ATO.
  • Estate planning? Facilitates tailored succession and pension planning. SMSF Association.
  • Pooling funds? Combine family funds for larger investments. ATO.
  • Retirement goals? Aligns investments with personal retirement objectives. SMSF Association.
  • Transparency? Full visibility of fund performance and fees. ATO.
  • Long-term savings? Encourages disciplined, long-term wealth building. SMSF Association.
  • Customisation? Tailor fund to unique financial circumstances. ATO.

How to Set Up an SMSF?

  • First step? Decide if SMSF suits your financial goals, knowledge. ATO.
  • Trust structure? Choose individual or corporate trustee structure. SMSF Association.
  • Trust deed? Draft a compliant trust deed with legal help. ATO.
  • Register with ATO? Apply for ABN and register with ATO. ATO.
  • Bank account? Open a dedicated SMSF bank account. SMSF Association.

  • Investment strategy? Develop and regularly review investment strategy. ATO.
  • Record-keeping? Maintain accurate records for all transactions. SMSF Association.
  • Annual reporting? Lodge annual return with ATO. ATO.
  • Audits? Arrange annual independent audit. SMSF Association.
  • Contributions? Monitor contribution caps to avoid penalties. ATO.
  • Pensions? Manage pension payments per ATO rules. SMSF Association.
