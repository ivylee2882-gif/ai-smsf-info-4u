Advantages & Disadvantages of SMSF

Advantages

  • Investment control: Choose investments like shares, property, or crypto. SMSF Association.
  • Tax strategies: Optimise tax via franking credits, deductions. ATO.
  • Flexibility: Tailor fund to personal financial goals. SMSF Association.
  • Cost-effective: Lower fees for high balances over $500,000. ATO.
  • Estate planning: Customised succession and pension options. SMSF Association.

Disadvantages

  • Time-intensive: Requires active management and compliance effort. ATO.
  • High costs: Expensive for low balances under $200,000. SMSF Association.
  • Compliance risk: Breaches can lead to penalties or disqualification. ATO.
  • Limited diversification: Smaller funds may struggle to diversify investments. SMSF Association.
  • Expertise needed: Requires financial knowledge or professional help. ATO.
