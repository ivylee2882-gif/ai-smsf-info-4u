Hot Court Cases Involving SMSF

Case 1: Non-Compliance with Investment Strategy

In 2023, an SMSF trustee was penalised for failing to adhere to the fund’s investment strategy, investing heavily in a single property without diversification. The ATO issued a $50,000 fine and disqualified the trustee. The case highlighted the importance of aligning investments with the documented strategy and maintaining diversification to mitigate risk. The trustee’s appeal was dismissed, reinforcing ATO’s strict oversight. ATO Case Reference.

Case 2: Breach of In-House Asset Rules

A 2024 case involved an SMSF lending to a related party, exceeding the 5% in-house asset limit. The ATO imposed penalties and ordered rectification. This case underscores the strict rules on in-house assets and the need for trustees to monitor related-party transactions to ensure compliance with SIS Act regulations. ATO Case Reference.
